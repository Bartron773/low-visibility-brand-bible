# AI Use and Provenance Policy

## Human Authorship and Direction

Projects in this repository may include work developed with assistance from generative-AI systems. Bart Salazar provides the originating concepts, creative direction, prompts, selection, evaluation, arrangement, editing, integration, and final approval unless a project states otherwise.

AI assistance does not mean the repository is unowned, public domain, freely reusable, or automatically open source. Rights are asserted in the human-authored code, text, direction, selection, arrangement, revisions, compilations, and other protectable expression, subject to applicable law and any third-party terms.

## Permitted Internal Uses

The Author may use AI systems for activities such as:

- ideation and exploratory drafting;
- coding assistance and debugging;
- visual experimentation and prototyping;
- editing, transformation, organization, and research support;
- accessibility, transcription, summarization, and production workflows.

AI-generated or AI-assisted material is reviewed, selected, modified, rejected, or incorporated under human editorial control.

## No AI Training or Dataset Permission

Public access to this repository does not grant permission to use its contents for:

- training, fine-tuning, reinforcement learning, distillation, grounding, retrieval augmentation, embedding collections, or model evaluation;
- dataset creation, scraping, bulk ingestion, synthetic-data generation, prompt libraries, style imitation, or benchmark construction;
- generation of substantially similar projects, campaigns, interfaces, visual systems, characters, narratives, or branded materials.

This restriction applies to automated and manual collection intended for machine-learning or generative-system development, except where such restriction is preempted or otherwise unenforceable under applicable law.

## Provenance and Disclosure

Where practical and relevant, project documentation may identify major AI tools or workflows used. Exact prompts, intermediate generations, private conversations, working files, and rejected outputs are not automatically part of the published record and need not be disclosed.

The absence of an AI disclosure does not establish that no AI assistance occurred. Likewise, an AI disclosure does not diminish the Author’s human contribution, editorial responsibility, or asserted rights.

## Accuracy and Responsibility

AI-assisted content may contain errors, omissions, artifacts, or invented details. Repository materials should not be treated as professional legal, medical, financial, engineering, scientific, or safety advice unless expressly identified and independently verified by a qualified professional.

## Third-Party Systems

AI tools, models, APIs, fonts, libraries, media, and platforms remain subject to their providers’ respective terms. References to those systems do not imply endorsement, sponsorship, partnership, or ownership.

## Requests

Requests to license repository content for research, datasets, model development, commercial use, publication, or collaboration must receive prior written permission from Bart Salazar.

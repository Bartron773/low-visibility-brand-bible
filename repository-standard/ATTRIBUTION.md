# Attribution and Brand-Use Standard

## Preferred Attribution

When reference, quotation, commentary, review, reporting, scholarship, or another legally permitted use identifies this work, use the following credit where practical:

> Created by Bart Salazar / Simple Minds Studios. Original source: the applicable Bartron773 GitHub repository.

For a specific project, include the project title and a direct link to its original repository or official project page.

Attribution is not a substitute for permission. Giving credit does not authorize copying, adaptation, redistribution, commercial use, dataset ingestion, or creation of derivative works.

## Do Not Imply Affiliation

Do not use the names Bart Salazar, Bartron773, Simple Minds Studios, project titles, collection names, logos, visual identities, or confusingly similar design elements in a way that suggests:

- sponsorship or endorsement;
- an official partnership or collaboration;
- authorship by, employment by, or affiliation with Bart Salazar or Simple Minds Studios;
- that a modified, copied, or derivative project is an official release.

## Original Source and Integrity

Do not remove, obscure, or alter authorship, copyright, provenance, watermark, metadata, license, or attribution notices.

Do not present screenshots, excerpts, prototypes, generated assets, source code, design systems, documentation, or concepts outside their original context in a materially misleading way.

## Project Names and Visual Identity

Project names, collection names, logos, campaign identities, and distinctive visual presentation may be protected by trademark, trade-dress, unfair-competition, contract, or other laws even where copyright does not apply to a name or short phrase by itself.

No trademark license is granted. Nominative reference for accurate identification, criticism, or reporting should use only what is reasonably necessary and should not imply endorsement.

## Collaborators and Third Parties

Where a project credits collaborators, photographers, artists, developers, AI providers, libraries, platforms, or other third parties, preserve those credits. Third-party marks and materials remain subject to their owners’ rules.

## Permission and Media Requests

For publication, exhibitions, press, licensing, commercial collaboration, adaptation, or high-resolution asset requests, contact Bart Salazar through the official Simple Minds Studios website or the GitHub account associated with the project.
